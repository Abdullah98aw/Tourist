Barzan Market

The market offers commodities and merchandise of all kinds, including women's and children's wear, perfumes, cosmetics, and food products, as well as perfumery products, furnishings, and household utensils. The market is also one of the most prominent markets in the region that visitors and tourists most visit, and it is the most crowded with shoppers, as it is the economic front for the region and the destination of many merchants.
